Who We Are

TenderCare Surrogacy Agency is a professionally registered and ethically driven surrogacy support agency committed to helping individuals and couples build families safely, legally, and with dignity.

As a CAC-registered organization, we operate with transparency, accountability, and a deep respect for human life. Our work goes beyond coordination — we provide guidance, emotional reassurance, structured processes, and trusted partnerships with certified fertility clinics and legal professionals.

Over the years, we have supported intended parents and surrogate mothers through one of the most sensitive journeys of their lives. Every step we take is rooted in compassion, confidentiality, and ethical responsibility.

At TenderCare, we believe surrogacy is not just a medical process — it is a deeply human experience that deserves care, clarity, and trust.
